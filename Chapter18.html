The “ display ” property specifies if/how an element is displayed.

The inline-block elements have both characteristics of inline and block elements.

They only occupy the space necessary for their contents so that they will not cause any line breaks.

The overflow property specifies how the overflowed content is rendered inside its containing element and use opacity.

The “ text-shadow ” property adds a shadow to text and accepts a comma-separated list of shadows.
